Who are we?

We are a team of [B.Sc|M.Sc.|Ph.D] students from Politecnico di Milano, with a lively interest in topics pertaining computer security, cryptography and general technical prowess. After taking part in a number of computer security contests, we had a sudden enlightenment and thought about organizing one ourselves :)
